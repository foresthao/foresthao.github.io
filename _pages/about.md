## About me

I am **Hao Yan**, a **Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science** at **Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), China** (09.2022 – Present). My research focuses on **cyber security** and **intrusion detection**, with an emphasis on **graph learning** for modeling network traffic and security events.

My recent work explores **graph-based representation learning** and **continual learning** for streaming intrusion detection, aiming to achieve robust detection under evolving attack patterns and limited replay budgets. I am also interested in bridging heterogeneous security data sources (e.g., network flows and host logs) to build unified detection pipelines that are both effective and practically deployable.

I am open to academic collaboration and discussion. If you would like to connect, please feel free to reach me via email (see the sidebar).
